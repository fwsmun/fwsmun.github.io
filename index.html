About FWSMUN

Our Mission

FWSMUN is Future World School's premier Model United Nations conference, dedicated to fostering diplomatic skills, critical thinking, and global awareness among students. Our conference provides a platform for young leaders to engage with pressing international issues and develop solutions through collaborative debate.

In its second iteration, FWSMUN has grown into one of the region's most respected MUN conferences, attracting delegates from schools across the country. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of academic rigor while creating an inclusive and supportive environment for all participants.
